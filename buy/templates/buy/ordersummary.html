About Us

GM Merchandise (GM) was founded as a shoe store by Jecee Ryn Obrero and Franz, Laconsay, in September 2020 in downtown Cebu. GM is now a leading wholesaler and distributor of general merchandise in the Philippines. With an average traffic of 4.2 million daily in the Philippines, 300,000 in China and over 20,000 product partners, GM Merchandise provides family fun experiences as it partners with the best-loved brands and events.




Mission Vision

GM Merchandise aims to become Asia’s most popular mall chain through constant innovation and partnership with the world’s best retailers. We provide the family fun mall experience that makes every day better for all.
